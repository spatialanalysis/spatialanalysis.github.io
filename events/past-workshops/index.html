Past Workshops

During the 2018-2019 school year, the Center for Spatial Data Science piloted the R Spatial Workshop for researchers, students, and staff. The workshops were run by Angela Li, R Spatial Advocate for the CSDS. An outline of the workshops that were held are included below, with links to the workshop notes from each session.

Topics covered during the workshops included:

  • Spatial data formats
  • Projections
  • Spatial data handling
  • Spatial data operations
  • Static maps
  • Interactive maps
  • Organization of research projects
  • Reproducible research with R
